How to read this board (30 seconds)
APEX 0.50 - the player's grade: the historical expectation for his draft slot, nudged halfway toward the model's own read. 0-100 percentile within his class; higher = better expected career.
Edge - where the model disagrees with the NFL. + green = model likes him more than his pick (potential steal). - red = model thinks he was over-drafted (bust risk).
Actual - for finished careers: where the player really ranked in his class, so you can check the model (and the market) against reality.
Trust level - the edge is a small, honestly validated signal (+0.013 average rank lift, 9 of 11 years). Strongest for late-round EDGE/RB flags; weakest for QBs. It will not out-forecast the whole first round.
